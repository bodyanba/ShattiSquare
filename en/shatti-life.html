Enhancing, augmenting and harmonising the various elements of this unique social hub, Omran is proud to introduce Shatti Square, a mixed-use development that takes premium urban living to an exciting new level of refinement and sophistication.

Shatti Square has been expressly designed and developed to offer a connected new approach to premium urban living, leisure and entertainment, taking the fabric of Oman’s enviable lifestyle to a new era of comfort and convenience.

OMRAN
MASTER DEVELOPER

Established in 2005 with a directive to assume responsibility for the critical transformation and sustainable development of Oman’s tourism infrastructure, OMRAN’s mandate has expanded and evolved to encompass the master development of major tourism and heritage destinations as well as innovative urban lifestyle developments, delivering some of the nation’s most iconic projects.

Respectful of the unique aspects of Oman’s culture nd heritage, OMRAN is fully committed to the long-term development of the local communities in which it operates, preserving Oman’s natural beauty and social fabric.
