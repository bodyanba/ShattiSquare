Residences
Step outside and walk along the endless Shatti Beach, stroll around the landscaped plaza or step into the world of drama and culture - whatever you choose to do, your options are many.

Desirable as homes, holiday residences or investment properties, these premium apartments enjoy a prime beachfront location in the heart of Muscat City, set alongside the majestic Royal Opera House and surrounded by well-established attractions and amenities.

Striking in their design and generously proportioned, these Signature Residences are characterised by superb design, finish and fixtures as well as first-class amenities which include generous parking, complementary valet service and over 30,000m2 of landscaped plaza.

For complete peace of mind, the residences have 24-hour security, concierge services, access to Muscat InterContinetal facilities and international standard maintenance.

THE PLAZA

The vibrant gateway to the Shatti Square lifestyle, The Plaza entwines and weaves together outdoor experiences to reimagine city living.

Central to the Shatti development, augmenting the characteristic appeal of the area and interconnecting its various elements, The Plaza offers a host of novel leisure facilities and amenities for the enjoyment of the community, visitors and tourists. These include luscious shaded walkways, dedicated jogging tracks, specially created gathering spaces for families and friends, and an open-air 100-seat amphitheatre – an exclusive new venue for memorable experiences.

Framing The Plaza is a carefully curated range of high-end dining and retail optionsincluding one-of-a-kind boutiques, galleries, ateliers and emporia. Harmonising leisure and entertainment experiences, The Plaza is connected to the Royal Opera House Muscat, via a dedicated walkway.

A selection of service outlets including a supermarket, gym, spa and beauty salon cater to the everyday needs of residents while a two-storey 1,300 space underground parking facility allows convenient access to all facilities.
