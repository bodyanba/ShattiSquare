Intercontinental Muscat
The InterContinental Muscat is the iconic heart of the capital city’s social fabric, the stately stage for sumptuous banquets and balls, lavish weddings and receptions, celebrations and festivities.

To introduce the much-loved hotel’s legacy of standard-setting luxury and enduring charm to a new generation of visitors and guests, a carefully crafted suite of refinements and enhancements has been developed.

Extended and redesigned, the Jabrin Ballroom will redefine elegance in the sultanate, its striking style echoed and amplified in the newly fitted guest rooms and suites.

Connecting the interior of the InterContinental Muscat to the stunning surrounding environment, the dual aspect lobby now opens to the sky and looks out to therefreshed garden landscape and, of course, Shatti Beach. State-of-the-art leisure facilities add new energy to the hotel ambience amongst them a five-star spa, first-class gym and an elevated pool area with uninterrupted horizon views. A sophisticated new club tower increases accommodation options in the hotel, bringing its total number of keys to 269.
